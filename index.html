About us

JRPASS is a UK-based company selling tickets for Japanese railway connections all around the world. You click, order, pay - and within a few days you have ticket arriving at your doorstep. With JRPass you can travel all around Japan or within its specific regions with just one ticket.

Challenge

We took the project over from the previous team. The process of transferring the work was challenging in itself. The system had to be continuously developed, while the developers and process were changing. Durning the development, we also encountered several challenges connected to performance and business optimalization.

The main ones where:

  • integrating the project into our development framework
  • lack of Regional Passes offers
  • lack of website translations to various popular languages
  • old versions of the framework
